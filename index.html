About me

I'm currently a post-doctoral fellow at City University of Hong Kong. I got my Ph.D. in Computer and Information Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, under the supervisor of Dr. Junting Chen. My research interest lies in matrix completion, tensor decomposition, generative models, with applications wireless communication and signal processing.
